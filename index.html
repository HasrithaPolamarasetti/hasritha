About

My journey into data analytics began with curiosity and the desire to understand why things happen, not just what happens. During my early engineering years, I realized that every meaningful decision, whether in technology or business, is ultimately powered by data. That realization became my career compass.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked across analytics and automation projects that blended SQL, Python, and cloud technologies like AWS Glue and Athena to turn messy datasets into actionable insights. From optimizing financial campaigns to building ML-driven dashboards, I’ve seen firsthand how well-structured data can transform strategy, efficiency, and customer experience.

Today, I combine data engineering, analytics, and AI to help teams make faster, more confident decisions. I thrive where technology meets impact, simplifying complexity, revealing trends, and creating systems that let data tell its story.

Outside of dashboards and pipelines, I’m continuously exploring emerging AI applications and how generative models can make analytics smarter and more human-centered.
